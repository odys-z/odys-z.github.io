A Introduction Driving Instruction Assistant System

What's it

This system have 2 pieces of equipment, a wearable camera and a Pad. Demo pictures are here (click for larger one).


When a driver preparing road tests, he can have an instructor in the car, using the Pad to record his operation errors while the camera is recording his own view. After the test, they can replay and analys the errors and correct the operations next time.
